
Shiny application to demonstrate the methods and reproduce results detailed in the article, "A multicriteria framework for disease surveillance site selection: case study for Plasmodium knowlesi malaria in Indonesia"

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data from the following publications are included in the application:

- Shearer, F.M., Huang, Z., Weiss, D.J., Wiebe, A., Gibson, H.S., Battle, K.E., Pigott, D.M., Brady, O.J., Putaporntip, C., Jongwutiwes, S. and Lau, Y.L., et al. Estimating geographical variation in the risk of zoonotic Plasmodium knowlesi infection in countries eliminating malaria. PLoS neglected tropical diseases, 10(8), p.e0004915 (2016). https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pntd.0004915

- Weiss, D., Nelson, A., Gibson, H. et al. A global map of travel time to cities to assess inequalities in accessibility in 2015. Nature 553, 333â€“336 (2018). http://doi.org/10.1038/nature25181
